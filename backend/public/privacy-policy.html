Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to JobNearMe ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Address and city (for employers)
  • Job-Related Information: Job applications, job postings, and related content

2.2 Location Data

With your permission, we collect your device's location to:

  • Show jobs near your current location
  • Display job locations on the map
  • Calculate distance to job opportunities
Note: You can disable location services at any time through your device settings. Some features may be limited without location access.

2.3 Device Information

We may collect device information including push notification tokens to send you relevant notifications about job opportunities and application updates.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process job applications and facilitate connections between job seekers and employers
  • Send notifications about new jobs, application status updates, and important announcements
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Employers: When you apply for a job, your profile information is shared with the relevant employer
  • With Job Seekers: Employers' job postings and basic contact information are visible to job seekers
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using HTTPS
  • Secure password hashing
  • Regular security assessments

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information through your profile settings
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications through app settings
To request account deletion, please visit: Delete Account

7. Children's Privacy

JobNearMe is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: contact@jobnearme.com
